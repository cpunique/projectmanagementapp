**[ATTORNEY TO COMPLETE - BE SPECIFIC]**

**Guidance for Attorney:**

### Strictly Necessary Cookies (No consent required)

**Purpose:** These cookies are essential for the Service to function. The Service cannot work without them.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|------------|---------|----------|------|
| `session_id` | Maintain user session | Session | First-party |
| `auth_token` | Keep you logged in | 30 days | First-party |
| `csrf_token` | Prevent security attacks | Session | First-party |
| `__firebase_*` | Firebase authentication | Varies | Third-party (Firebase) |

**Cannot be disabled:** These cookies are necessary for security and functionality.

### Functional/Preference Cookies (May require consent in some jurisdictions)

**Purpose:** Remember your preferences and settings.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|------------|---------|----------|------|
| `dark_mode` | Remember dark mode preference | 1 year | First-party |
| `language` | Remember language preference | 1 year | First-party |
| `panel_state` | Remember UI panel states | 1 year | First-party |
| `kanban-store` | Store board data locally | Persistent | Local Storage |

**User control:** You can manage these via cookie settings.

### Analytics/Performance Cookies (Consent required)

**Purpose:** Help us understand how you use the Service so we can improve it.

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|------------|----------|---------|----------|------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguish users | 2 years | Third-party |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Session state | 2 years | Third-party |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Distinguish users | 24 hours | Third-party |

**User control:** You can opt-out of analytics cookies in cookie settings.

**Data collected:**

- Features used
- Time spent on Service
- Device and browser information

## 7. How to Manage Cookies

**[ATTORNEY TO COMPLETE]**

**Guidance for Attorney:**

### Browser Controls

**Chrome:**
1. Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preference
3. Manage exceptions

**Firefox:**
1. Settings > Privacy & Security
2. Cookies and Site Data section
3. Manage permissions

**Safari:**
1. Preferences > Privacy
2. Cookie blocking options
3. Manage website data

**Edge:**
1. Settings > Cookies and site permissions
2. Manage and delete cookies

**Mobile devices:**
- iOS: Settings > Safari > Block Cookies
- Android: Browser settings > Privacy > Clear cookies

### Effect of Disabling Cookies

**Essential cookies:**
- Service will not work properly
- Cannot stay logged in
- Security features disabled

**Other cookies:**
- Service will still work
- Some features may be limited
- Preferences won't be remembered
- Analytics disabled (we can't improve Service based on usage)
